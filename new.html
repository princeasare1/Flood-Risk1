Flood Prone Areas in Greater Accra

Be extra cautious when traveling through these areas during heavy downpours

Odaw River Basin

Frequent flooding due to blocked drainage systems and encroachment on the river's natural course.

Accra Central Business District

Vulnerable to flash floods due to inadequate drainage infrastructure and high urbanization.

Tema Metropolitan Area

Coastal flooding risks combined with urban drainage challenges in low-lying areas.

Ablekuma

High vulnerability due to informal settlements and poor drainage systems.

Chemu-Lagoon

Chemu-Lagoon is fierced with improper waste disposal, causing blockage in the drainage system. This puts settlem around the lagoon at risk of flooding.

Mallam Junction

Coastal erosion and storm surge risks combined with urban flooding challenges.

Flood Preparedness Information

Greater Accra Region faces significant flood risks, particularly during the rainy season. Key factors contributing to flooding include:

  • Inadequate drainage infrastructure
  • Encroachment on watercourses and wetlands
  • Rapid urbanization outpacing infrastructure development
  • Poor waste management blocking drainage systems
  • Climate change intensifying rainfall patterns

Stay informed and prepared by monitoring weather alerts and following evacuation guidelines when necessary.
